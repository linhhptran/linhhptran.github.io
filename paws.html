The Purr-fect Friend for Your Pet

I grew up with two dogs, so being around animals has always felt natural. I started petsitting by watching dogs for friends, and over time, word spread — soon I was caring for dogs of friends of friends and Amazonians.

Today, I offer housesitting, boarding, daycare, and overnight stays. I take dogs on hikes, to parks, to the office, or simply keep them company at home, depending on what they need. Unless otherwise requested, I only take dogs from the same family at the same time to ensure they’re comfortable and safe.

I've worked with all kinds of dogs — puppies, seniors, reactive dogs, dogs with separation anxiety — from extra small breeds to large ones.

Each one has their own story, and I’m here to make sure they’re safe, happy, and loved while their humans are away.
